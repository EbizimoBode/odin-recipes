Omelette du fomage

Description

Eggs made with goat cheese! Yum. A fascinating combo

The result will leave you feeling full and fufilled and its quite easy to make

Ingredients.

  • 3 eggs.
  • A small wedge of goat cheese.
  • Parsley flakes.
  • 2 cloves of garlic.
  • 1/4 cup of condensed milk.
  • 1/4 cup of butter.
  • 1 tbsp of salt.

Let's break a few eggs

  1. Crack the eggs and whisk in a bowl.
  2. Pour the condensed milk into the egg, add your salt and mix.
  3. Grate the cheese into the bowl of eggs and mix.
  4. Grate the garlic, grease a small frying pan with the butter and fry the garlic for a minute.
  5. Pour the egg and cheese mixture into the frying, shake a few parsley flakes on top.
  6. Fry until the underneath is done or is slightly brown, flip and fry the otherside.
  7. Enjoy with bread.
